Yerupajá

Perú's Most Spectacular Mountain

Bolognesi, Ancash

Yerupajá, which means 'white morning' in the local Quechua language, is the second-highest mountain in Perú and the main peak of the Huayhuash range.

Some other peaks of the Huayhuash range are:

  • Siula
  • Sarapo
  • Jiricashca
  • Yerupajá Chico
  • Rasac

Including Yerupajá, these are the peaks that are higher than 6,000m (19,685ft).
